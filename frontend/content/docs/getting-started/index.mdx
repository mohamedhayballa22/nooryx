Unlike legacy ERPs that require weeks of implementation, Nooryx is designed to be configured in minutes. However, because we enforce strict auditability, the initial setup requires a few deliberate decisions.

## Chapters

This section outlines the foundational steps to set up your organization, structured into three chapters.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **<Link href="/docs/getting-started/workspace">Create Your Workspace:</Link>**
    Set up your organization and configure your primary workspace settings.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **<Link href="/docs/getting-started/team">Invite Your Team:</Link>**
    Bring your team into your workspace so everyone can start collaborating in Nooryx.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **<Link href="/docs/getting-started/first-transaction">Log First Transaction:</Link>**
    Record your first inventory movement to bring your workspace to life.
  </Step>
</Steps>
